Ingrediants :-


  • 8 oz. chow mein noodles

  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced into thin strips

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage

  • 1 cup sliced carrots

  • Fenugreek seeds

  • 1/2 cup sliced onion

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste



STEP BY STEP METHOD :-


  1. Cook the chow mein noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.

  2. In a large wok or skillet, heat the vegetable oil over high heat.

  3. Add the sliced chicken breast and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.

  4. Add the shredded cabbage, sliced carrots, sliced bell peppers, onion, and minced garlic to the wok, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.

  5. Add the cooked chow mein noodles to the wok, along with the soy sauce and oyster sauce, and stir-fry everything together for another 2-3 minutes until well combined and heated through.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve hot.
